# Work Objectives and Key Metrics

## Objective 1: Data Infrastructure & System Reliability

Improve DG execution velocity through reliable systems and data infrastructure that creates accurate attribution and efficient campaign operations.

### Goals
* Ensure 95% data consistency across Marketo-SFDC-Airtable for campaign metadata and performance data
* Implement automated data quality monitoring with weekly scorecards tracking critical metrics
* Reduce campaign setup-to-launch time by 30% through standardized workflows
* Maintain published SLAs in Jira Service Management

### Key Metric: Data Consistency Score
Foundation for accurate attribution and reporting

**Targets:**
* Maintain data integrity across systems to ensure accuracy and consistency
* 95% on-time delivery for DG campaign build tickets
* 30% reduction in campaign setup time

---

## Objective 2: AI Workflow Adoption

Scale AI workflows across DG and MOps to boost productivity, targeting precision, and unlock new pipeline opportunities. Striving for 100% AI adoption across the DG team.

### Goals
* Launch 4 production AI workflows (Plan on a Page, persona research, campaign brief generator, post-event analysis)
* Train 100% of DG team on AI workflow usage with documentation
* AI tool pilots through experimentation fund (depending on budget)
* Build opportunity for Marketing team to share AI insights

### Key Metric: Team AI Adoption Rate
Enables productivity and velocity gains

**Targets:**
* 100%+ team adoption of AI workflows
* Quarterly showcases of AI technology use across Marketing as Polish week

---

## Objective 3: Workflow Automation

Automate repetitive workflows to free 20% of MOps and DG capacity for strategic initiatives.

### Goals
* Automate high-volume manual processes (e.g. list imports, MQL/MSL updates, campaign syncing, post-event reconciliation)
* Build campaign process monitoring system - to better speed up and prevent issues
* Own and create self-service documentation hub
* Architect automation solutions

### Key Metric: Capacity Increase
Frees time for strategic initiatives

**Targets:**
* 20% capacity increase through automation
* Maintain SLA x% achievement
* Increase of documentation engagement
* Automated alerts for operational issues
* Support x# of campaigns
